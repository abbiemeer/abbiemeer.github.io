College Learnings

Respect for Personal Time
It is important to respect everyone's personal time. Every weekend I make it a point to rest and enjoy my time with my family. It is the only time I am with them after being in Manila for a week.

Take a Pause
I always believe that a messy mind can result to a messy output. Whenever I am stressed, I make sure to breathe. If I continue to work when I'm stressed it will just result to an output that I will regret doing.

Only say YES if you can
You do not have to say yes to everything. You can always reflect and think things through. It is okay to say NO if you cannot do whatever is being asked you to do.

Its okay to make mistakes
I remember crying multiple times because I failed or made a mistake. But then I keep on reminding myself to learn from it and as much as possible never do it again. Failing or making mistakes is part of learning. Its okay, we are all learning.
